## ⚫ PRIORITY 7 — Link-Earning Tactics (Real Editorial Backlinks)

These take effort but earn the HIGHEST value backlinks:

1. **Guest posts** on local MD blogs (Baltimore Magazine, Bethesda Magazine, Annapolis travel blogs). Offer "Top 10 Things to Do in Annapolis" with a mention/link to your service.
2. **Press releases** announcing fleet additions, community involvement, awards — distribute via:
   - [ ] PRLog (free)
   - [ ] PRWeb (paid, higher authority)
   - [ ] eReleases
   - [ ] Local MD news outlets
3. **Sponsor local MD events/charities** — most sponsorships include a website link. Look at:
   - [ ] USNA Commissioning Week events
   - [ ] Annapolis Boat Show
   - [ ] Preakness events
   - [ ] Local prom fundraisers
4. **Partner with MD hotels** (Gaylord National, The Westin Annapolis, Hyatt Regency Bethesda) — ask to be their "preferred transportation partner" with a link on their concierge page.
5. **Partner with MD wedding venues** — mutual backlink exchange or "preferred vendor" listings:
   - The Tidewater Inn, The Oaks Waterfront, Chesapeake Bay Beach Club, Belvedere Hotel Baltimore.
6. **HARO (Help A Reporter Out)** — https://www.helpareporter.com — respond to journalist queries about travel, transportation, MD business.
7. **Scholarship offer page** on your own site (`/scholarship`) — .edu links from MD universities are extremely valuable.
